About BugOff

I once dreamed I was wiping out bugs — and woke up grinning. That feeling? I wanted to share it. So I built BugOff: a goofy, stress-relief game made by a dev, for devs.

No real bugs hurt. No animals harmed. Just virtual icons and pixels. Grab them, drag them to the fire, watch them burn. It’s oddly satisfying.

Life’s short. Sometimes you need a silly break. This is it. Enjoy. 🔥
